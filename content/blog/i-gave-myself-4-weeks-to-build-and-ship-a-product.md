## The idea

I wanted to build something for quite some time, but I never put enough time into it and I was always overthinking every step, especially the first one. I had no idea what to build and I find this step really hard.

I had few simple ideas, but for every one I was able to find a million problems why they wouldn't work. So time has passed and I was postponing a lot, up untill now where I found myself in a good spot personally and with a new boost of motivation. Now I want to capture this opportunity and build anything, so I've been thinking to just pick a product that alraedy works and is relatively easy to build in 4 weeks. I'll have a hard deadline to ship it and that's the main goal. I won't bother with the stuff afterwards. Even if it fails I'll be learning a lot and maybe a new idea will be born in the process.

So without much thinking this time I chose to build a feature flag platform. Simple enough (at least the basic features) with quite some interesting challenges. The market looks pretty crowded and I have no idea how to eventually sell this, but that's the point now, I'll think about it when and if the time comes.
